Plant Layout

Our plant configured with 2 spray paint lines and 1 secondary element & electric apparatus installation line. It is also the first large set of continuous sandblasting coating production line in Taiwan.

Our sandblasting coating production line runs by continuous production method, the total length of one line is 90m(L)*30m(W)*25m (H), and the other 60m(L)*25m(W)*15m(H). The sandblasting and coating areas are separated by compartment sliding doors, in order to prevent cross contamination. Both lines are configured with sandblasting and blasting sand recycle equipment, dust collecting equipment, dehumidification equipment, drying device for compressed air and VOCs pollution prevention equipment.

The spec of our secondary element & electric apparatus installation line is 140m(L)*25m(W)*30m(H), we can provide installation works for internal & external platforms, Davit crane, switch box, control penal, cable, cable rack, lighting, socket and other equipment or Free issue items provided by other owners.
